# Structural Restoration for Heritage Properties

Address underlying structural issues in your period home while preserving its historical character. Our specialists understand how to strengthen and repair heritage structures using sympathetic methods.

## Common Structural Issues in Heritage Homes:

*   **Settled Foundations and Footings**
*   **Decayed Floor Framing and Joists**
*   **Sagging Roof Structures**
*   **Wall Tie Replacement in Double Brick**
*   **Borer Damage in Structural Timbers**

## Our Structural Restoration Methodology

1.  **Engineering Assessment:** Working with heritage engineers to diagnose structural issues and plan appropriate repairs.
2.  **Minimal Intervention:** Designing solutions that provide necessary strength with the least impact on original fabric.
3.  **Traditional Materials:** Using appropriate materials like seasoned hardwoods and compatible mortars.
4.  **Sympathetic Strengthening:** Techniques that reinforce without compromising historical significance.
5.  **Council Compliance:** Navigating heritage approvals and ensuring all work meets conservation standards.
