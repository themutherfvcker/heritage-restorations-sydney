# Expert Sandstone Repair & Restoration for Heritage Properties

Sydney's iconic sandstone buildings require specialist care to maintain their structural integrity and historic character. Our stonework experts preserve the beauty and strength of your heritage property.

## Common Sandstone Problems We Address:

*   **Crumbling and Eroding Stone Surfaces**
*   **Cracked or Displaced Sandstone Blocks**
*   **Deteriorated Lime Mortar Joints**
*   **Salt Damp and Efflorescence**
*   **Biological Growth and Staining**

## Our Sandstone Restoration Approach

1.  **Stone Analysis:** We assess the type and condition of your sandstone to determine the appropriate restoration methods.
2.  **Mortar Matching:** Using traditional lime-based mortars that match the original in composition and colour.
3.  **Stone Repair:** Carefully repairing damaged sections using like-for-like sandstone where replacement is necessary.
4.  **Cleaning & Conservation:** Gentle cleaning methods that preserve the stone's patina while removing harmful deposits.
5.  **Protective Treatments:** Applying appropriate breathable coatings to protect against future weathering.
