# Decorative Plasterwork Restoration

Preserve the intricate interior details that define your heritage home's character. Our plasterwork specialists repair and replicate ornamental plaster features using traditional methods and materials.

## Plasterwork Services:

*   **Cornice and Coving Repair & Replication**
*   **Ceiling Rose Restoration**
*   **Ornamental Plaster Panel Repair**
*   **Traditional Lime Plaster Wall Finishes**
*   **Plaster Ceiling Stabilisation**

## Our Plasterwork Restoration Process

1.  **Mould Making:** Creating silicone moulds from existing details to ensure accurate replication.
2.  **Traditional Materials:** Using lime-based plasters and traditional binders for historical accuracy.
3.  **Artisan Craftsmanship:** Hand-finishing and tooling to match original workmanship.
4.  **Seamless Repair:** Blending new work with existing plaster for invisible repairs.
5.  **Structural Support:** Ensuring plaster details are properly supported and secured.
