## Understanding Paddington's Heritage

Paddington's terraces feature distinctive elements that require specialist care:

*   **Original Welsh Slate Roofs** hidden beneath later corrugated iron
*   **Shell-lime Mortar** in original brickwork that requires specific repointing techniques
*   **Cast Iron Lacework** that needs careful restoration and replication
*   **Timber Floor Systems** that may need reinforcement while preserving original boards

## Woollahra Council Heritage Compliance

Our specialists are familiar with Woollahra Council's heritage overlay and can help navigate:

*   **Development Application (DA) requirements** for heritage properties
*   **Materials and colour approvals** for external works
*   **Conservation management plans** for significant properties
*   **Heritage impact statements** for major restoration projects
