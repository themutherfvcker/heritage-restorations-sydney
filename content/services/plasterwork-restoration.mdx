## The Art of Ornamental Plaster

Decorative plasterwork represents the pinnacle of heritage interior craftsmanship. From the elaborate cornices of Victorian drawing rooms to the elegant ceiling roses of Federation halls, each plaster detail tells a story of artistic skill and architectural ambition that demands specialist knowledge to preserve.

### Understanding Period Plaster Styles

Sydney's heritage interiors followed distinct plaster traditions that our specialists understand:

- **Victorian High Style**: Elaborate cornices, deep mouldings, complex ceiling roses
- **Federation Simplicity**: Softer profiles, Australian motifs, Arts & Crafts influence
- **Colonial Practicality**: Simple cornices, minimal ornamentation, practical detailing
- **Traditional Composition**: Lime-based plasters, hair reinforcement, hand-tooled finishes

Each plaster style requires specific repair, replication, and finishing techniques that match original workmanship and material composition.
