## Common Heritage Roof Problems We Specialize In

### Structural Issues
*   **Sagging Roof Plane** - Often caused by timber batten decay or wall plate deterioration
*   **Valley Board Rot** - Hidden structural damage requiring careful access and repair
*   **Flashing Failure** - Original lead or zinc flashings that have deteriorated

### Material-Specific Problems
*   **Terracotta Tile Slippage** - Caused by failed mortar bedding in Federation homes
*   **Slate Delamination** - Welsh slate splitting along natural cleavage lines
*   **Iron Corrosion** - Original corrugated iron suffering from underside condensation

### Water Management Failures
*   **Valley Leaks** - Complex intersections requiring traditional folded metal techniques
*   **Parapet Wall Penetration** - Water tracking through original brickwork
*   **Verge Deterioration** - Mortar erosion at gable ends

## Sydney Council Heritage Requirements

### Woollahra Council Standards
*   **Material Retention** - Minimum 80% original material preservation required
*   **Colour Compliance** - Heritage palette restrictions for all visible elements
*   **Profile Matching** - Tile profiles must match original dimensions
*   **Mortar Specification** - Non-portland cement, lime-based mixes only

### Inner West Council Guidelines
*   **Character Statement** - Required for all heritage restoration applications
*   **Material Sampling** - Physical samples required for council approval
*   **Craftsmanship Standards** - Traditional techniques must be demonstrated
*   **Documentation** - Detailed photographic evidence throughout process

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does heritage roof restoration take?
Most projects require 4-8 weeks, with additional time for council approvals. Emergency repairs can be completed in 1-2 weeks.

### Can original materials be reused?
In most cases, 70-90% of original materials can be preserved and reused with careful restoration techniques.

### Do I need council approval?
Yes, all external changes to heritage properties require council approval, even like-for-like repairs.

### How do you match original tiles?
We maintain relationships with reclamation yards and specialty manufacturers to source period-appropriate replacements.
